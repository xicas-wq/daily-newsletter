## Energy Transition: New Vulnerabilities

A notable analysis from Project Syndicate warns that the **clean energy transition won't eliminate geopolitical risk — it will just redistribute it**. While renewables reduce dependence on oil chokepoints like the Strait of Hormuz, they create new bottlenecks in:
- **Critical mineral supply chains** (lithium, cobalt, rare earths)
- **Refining and processing** (dominated by China)
- **Battery manufacturing** (concentrated in a few countries)

The current oil crisis is a preview: nations dependent on one energy source are scrambling. Diversification is the lesson.
